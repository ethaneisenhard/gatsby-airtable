Paprika Parmesan Chicken

Preparation Time: 15

Total Time: 45

Ingredients: 4 Skinless boneless chicken breasts trimmed, 1/2 CUP Parmesan cheese grated, 2 tsp Paprika, 1/4 CUP All-purpose flour, 1/2 tsp Salt, 1/2 tsp Pepper, 1/2 tsp Garlic powder, 2 Eggs beaten, 1/4 CUP Butter melted

Directions: Preheat oven to 400Â°F. Set up 2 medium sized bowls. Beat 2 eggs in first bowl. Mix flour, parmesan, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a second bowl. Dip chicken, 1 breast at a time, in egg mixture, then in parmesan/paprika mixture, turning to evenly coat both sides of each breast with each ingredient. Place on prepared baking sheet, in a single layer. Pour the melted butter evenly over the chicken. Bake for about 40-45 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cheese has browned, and the chicken has cooked. Serve with broccoli or vegetable of choice.
